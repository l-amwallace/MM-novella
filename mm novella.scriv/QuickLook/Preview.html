  • 1D: THEY CAN’T AVOID EACH OTHER

    1D: THEY CAN’T AVOID EACH OTHER

    Although C1 and C2 might have no desire to pursue a relationship together, something still forces them to spend time together. Maybe they’re fake-dating, stuck in a snowstorm in the same cottage, their competing bookstores are going after the same customers or maybe they keep owing favours to each other (like in I Owe You One by Sophie Kinsella). Whatever it is, it’s your convenient excuse to put these two dummies together in a way that they can’t avoid.
